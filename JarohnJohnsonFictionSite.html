Jarohn C. Johnson

Bio

Jarohn C. Johnson is an emerging writer born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri. He graduated from the University of Missouri-St. Louis (UMSL) in 2019, where he received an MFA in Creative Writing. During his tenure at UMSL, he worked as an assistant editor of Natural Bridge Magazine and was awarded the Mary Troy Prize in Fiction.
